    • Game rules
  • 1. Each player is allowed 2 airlines/accounts. Patreon supporters are allowed an additional airline/account. Multiple accounts above the allowed number may result in suspension of all the accounts belonging to that player.
  • 2. Players may not use any of their airlines to target or harass another airline at their own detriment. Make sustainable business decisions.
  • 3. Offensive airline names and offensive chat messages are not allowed.
  • 4. Report any bugs or exploits in the discord, do not abuse the bug or exploit.
  • 5. Bots that do "read" operations should get approval from admins; Bots that perform "write" operations such as adjusting price, buying oil contracts are strictly prohibited. Any involved accounts will be subjected to permanent ban.
  • 6. Action taken regarding these rules is at the discretion of the game admins. If you feel like any action taken was incorrect, you may open a ticket in the discord #ban-appeals channel.
